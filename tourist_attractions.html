City Beach Park

A lovely stretch of sand, plunked alongside the water that surrounds downtown Sandpoint. According to local folklore,
the sand from City Beach wasn't naturally occuring- all the other beaches have pretty big rocks along their shore. So
some person or other had the idea to make it all a bit more... "people-friendly". Brought in dump truck loads of sand,
evened it out, slapped a park on it, and boom- happy SUMMER tourists, too! However, I tried to fact-check this one, and
came up empty-handed. So take it or leave it, lol
